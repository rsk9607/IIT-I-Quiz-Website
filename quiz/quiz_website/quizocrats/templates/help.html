How to write a quiz?

In the home page, you will find some tabs showing links to write different quizzes. If you are new to this website, check out the quiz format by attending a sample quiz. You can access to it by clicking Sample Quiz. You will be directed to the quiz page.

Now, you might have got familiar with our quiz format. You can access more quizzes in Quizzes tab. Also, Quizzes are segregated by their topics. you can look over it by clicking Tags icon.

Do you want to check results?

In this website, You can also check results of quizzes you gave. You can go to Resultstab and can check results.
